# MixClub User Guide

## 🎵 Welcome to MixClub!

Your all-in-one music production platform for mixing, mastering, collaboration, and distribution.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. **Create Your Account**
1. Visit [MixClub](/)
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
3. Choose your role:
   - **Artist**: I create music and need engineering services
   - **Engineer**: I provide mixing/mastering services
4. Complete the onboarding process

### 2. **Complete Your Profile**
- Add a profile picture
- Write a bio
- Set your musical preferences (genres, skills, etc.)
- For Engineers: Add your portfolio and credentials

---

## 👤 For Artists

### Creating Your First Project

1. Go to **Artist CRM** → **Active Work**
2. Click **"New Project"**
3. Fill in project details:
   - Project name
   - Description
   - Genre
   - Budget
   - Timeline
4. Upload your audio files
5. Click **"Create Project"**

### Finding an Engineer

1. Navigate to **Opportunities** tab
2. Browse our **AI-Powered Engineer Matching**:
   - Input your genre, budget, and urgency
   - Get personalized engineer recommendations
3. Or browse **All Engineers** manually
4. View engineer profiles:
   - Rating & reviews
   - Portfolio
   - Pricing
   - Availability

### Booking a Session

1. Select an engineer
2. Click **"Book Session"** or **"Post Job"**
3. Describe your requirements
4. Set your budget
5. Submit your application
6. Wait for engineer to accept

### Collaborating in the Studio

Once a session is booked:

1. Go to **Studio** tab
2. Join the live collaboration session
3. **Available Features:**
   - 🎤 Audio/Video chat
   - 🖥️ Screen sharing
   - 💬 Real-time chat
   - 🎚️ Audio mixer controls
   - 📝 Session comments with timestamps

### Payment & Checkout

1. Choose a service:
   - **Mixing Package**: Professional mixing services
   - **Mastering Package**: AI-powered mastering
   - **Distribution**: Release your music
2. Select package tier (Basic, Pro, Premium)
3. Enter payment details:
   - Credit/Debit Card (Stripe)
   - PayPal
   - Cryptocurrency
4. Complete purchase
5. Access unlocked immediately

### Reviewing Your Engineer

After project completion:

1. You'll receive a notification to rate your engineer
2. Rate 1-5 stars ⭐
3. Write a detailed review
4. Submit feedback
5. Engineer receives bonus for high ratings (4.5+)

## 🎧 For Engineers

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** tab
2. **Complete Your Profile:**
   - Professional bio
   - Years of experience
   - Genres & specialties
   - Equipment & DAW
   - Portfolio samples
3. **Set Your Rates:**
   - Hourly rate
   - Project-based pricing
   - Add-on services

### Finding Work

1. Navigate to **Opportunities**
2. Browse **Recommended Artists** (AI-matched)
3. Or view **All Open Jobs**
4. Filter by:
   - Genre
   - Budget
   - Timeline
   - Location

### Applying to Jobs

1. Click on a job posting
2. Review project details
3. Click **"Apply"**
4. Write a compelling proposal:
   - Your experience
   - Relevant portfolio pieces
   - Timeline estimate
   - Any questions
5. Submit application
6. Artist reviews and accepts

### Managing Your Work

**Active Work Tab:**
- **Tasks**: View all assigned tasks
  - Priority levels
  - Due dates
  - Project association
- **Active Sessions**: Your current projects
  - Client information
  - Project status
  - Time tracking

**Task Management:**
1. Select a task
2. Click **"Start"** to begin
3. Update status to **"In Progress"**
4. Complete task and mark **"Completed"**
5. Earn points and level up! 🎮

### Live Collaboration

1. Go to **Studio** tab
2. Start or join a session
3. **Studio Tools:**
   - 🎚️ Audio mixer controls
   - 🎤 Mic input (high-quality streaming)
   - 📹 Video sharing
   - 🖥️ Screen sharing
   - 💬 Session chat
   - 📁 File sharing
4. Record session if needed

### Earnings & Payouts

**Business Tab:**

**Earnings Overview:**
- Total earned
- Pending payments
- Available for payout
- Bonuses earned

**Payout Management:**
1. Click **"Request Payout"**
2. Enter amount (minimum $50)
3. Select payout method:
   - Bank transfer
   - PayPal
   - Cryptocurrency
4. Submit request
5. Receive payment within 3-5 business days

**Performance Bonuses:**
- High ratings (4.5+): +5% bonus
- Streak bonuses: Daily activity rewards
- Monthly awards: Top earner, top-rated, etc.

## ❓ FAQ

### For Everyone

**Q: Is MixClub free?**
A: Yes! Basic features are free. Premium services require subscriptions.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I use MixClub on mobile?**
A: Yes! Download our mobile app or use the PWA.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use Stripe for secure payment processing.

### For Artists

**Q: How do I find the right engineer?**
A: Use our AI-powered matching system or browse engineer profiles manually.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the final mix?**
A: Most engineers offer revisions. Discuss expectations upfront.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, anytime. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
A: Varies by complexity. Most mixing projects: 3-7 days. Mastering: 1-3 days.

### For Engineers

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Request payouts via bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency. Minimum: $50.

**Q: When do I receive payment?**
A: Artists pay upfront. Funds released when project is completed and approved.

**Q: How do I improve my ranking?**
A: Complete projects on time, maintain high ratings, stay active daily.

**Q: Can I work with artists off-platform?**
A: We encourage keeping work on MixClub to protect both parties and maintain quality.
