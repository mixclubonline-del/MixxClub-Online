## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account information | While account active + 30 days | Service provision |
| Audio files | Project completion + 90 days | Revisions and disputes |
| Transaction records | 7 years | Tax and legal compliance |
| Chat messages | 90 days | Support and disputes |
| Analytics data | 24 months (anonymized) | Service improvement |
| Audit logs | 180 days | Security and compliance |
| Deleted account data | 30 days (then permanent) | Recovery grace period |

### 5.2 Data Deletion

**Automatic Deletion:**
- Inactive accounts after 2 years (with notice)
- Completed project files after 90 days
- Old chat messages after 90 days
- Expired subscription data

**User-Requested Deletion:**
- Account deletion: Data removed within 30 days
- Specific file deletion: Immediate removal
- Right to erasure (GDPR): Processed within 30 days

**Exceptions:**
- Data required for legal compliance (7 years)
- Anonymized analytics (cannot identify users)
- Public content (reviews, forum posts) may be retained
